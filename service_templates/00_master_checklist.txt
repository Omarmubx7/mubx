==============================================================================
MASTER PROTOCOL: PRE-FLIGHT, HANDOFF & MAINTENANCE (ALL PROJECTS)
==============================================================================

Use this checklist for EVERY project type to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. LEGAL & COMPLIANCE (PROTECT YOURSELF & CLIENT)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] CONTRACT / AGREEMENT
    - [ ] Scope of Work signed (Deliverables clearly defined).
    - [ ] Payment Terms agreed (e.g., 50% Upfront, 50% on Delivery).
    - [ ] "Scope Creep" clause included (Extra work = Extra pay).

[ ] SITE LEGAL PAGES
    - [ ] Privacy Policy (Required if collecting emails/analytics).
    - [ ] Terms of Service.
    - [ ] Refund Policy (CRITICAL for E-commerce/Web Systems).
    - [ ] Cookie Consent Banner (if using tracking pixels/GA4).

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. CLIENT EDUCATION & HANDOFF
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] RECORDED TRAINING (LOOM)
    - [ ] "How to edit text/images" (if CMS used).
    - [ ] "How to view form submissions/leads".
    - [ ] "How to view analytics".
    - [ ] Keep video under 10 mins.

[ ] RECURRING COSTS SHEET
    - [ ] Create a PDF listing all monthly costs for the client:
        - Domain: ~$15/year
        - Vercel: Free (Hobby) or $20/mo (Pro).
        - Database (Supabase): Free or $25/mo.
        - Third-party APIs (Resend, Sanity, etc.).

[ ] CREDENTIALS TRANSFER
    - [ ] Securely share passwords (use 1Password link or similar).
    - [ ] Transfer Vercel/Domain ownership to Client's account (or keep on yours with maintenance fee).

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. QUALITY ASSURANCE (QA) - THE "OOPS" LIST
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] ERROR HANDLING
    - [ ] Custom `not-found.tsx` (404 Page) created?
    - [ ] Custom `error.tsx` (500 Page) created?
    - [ ] "Empty States" designed? (e.g., No blog posts found).

[ ] BROWSER & DEVICE TESTING
    - [ ] Tested on Safari (iOS)? (Often breaks flexbox/gradients).
    - [ ] Tested on Chrome (Android)?
    - [ ] Tested on Firefox?

[ ] FORM SPAM PROTECTION
    - [ ] Honeypot field hidden in forms?
    - [ ] Rate limiting enabled?

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. POST-LAUNCH SURVIVAL
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[ ] MONITORING
    - [ ] Uptime Robot (Free) setup to ping site every 5 mins.
    - [ ] Sentry (Free tier) connected for error logging.

[ ] BACKUPS
    - [ ] Database Backups scheduled (Daily/Weekly).
    - [ ] CMS Layout/Content export saved locally.

[ ] ASK FOR REVIEW
    - [ ] Send follow-up email 1 week later asking for a Google Review/Testimonial.
