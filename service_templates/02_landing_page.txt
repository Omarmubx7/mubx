2. **Hero Section**:

3. **Social Proof**: 'Trusted by' logos or stats strip.
4. **Benefits/Features**: 3-4 cards with icons explaining the 'Why'.
5. **Testimonials**: Carousel or grid of client reviews.
6. **FAQ**: Accordion for objective handling.
7. **Final CTA**: Simple form or button to close the deal.

# Functional Requirements
1. **Speed**: Use `next/font` and strictly optimized images. No CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift).
2. **Form**: On submit, send email via Resend and show a success specific message or toast.
3. **Tracking**: Create a `Link` wrapper or `Button` wrapper that fires a 'conversion' event to console (placeholder for GA4).

# File Structure Preference
- `src/app/page.tsx` (Landing Page layout)
- `src/components/sections/hero.tsx`
- `src/components/sections/features.tsx`
